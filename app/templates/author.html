About the Author

Daniel Welsch is an accomplished author, blogger, podcaster, and travel writer. Originally from Phoenix, Arizona, USA, Daniel now resides in Barcelona, Spain. He is passionate about teaching English and helping learners around the world improve their language skills.

Daniel has authored several books on English grammar and language learning, including the popular "La Guía de los Tiempos Verbales". He also runs educational blogs such as madridingles.net and aprendemasingles.com, where he shares valuable resources and tips for Spanish speakers learning English.

In addition to his writing, Daniel hosts the "Aprende Más Inglés" podcast, which has over 300 episodes, and occasionally creates content on YouTube. His work has been featured in notable publications like Esquire and Lonely Planet.

For more information about Daniel and his projects, you can visit his website danielwelsch.
